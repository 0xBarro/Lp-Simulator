Liquidity Pool Simulator

This page contains an implementation the pricing mechanism of Liquidity Pools (LPs) used by Automated Market Makers (AMMs) in DEFI applications.
The main purpose of this page is to provide an easy and interactable way to visualize and understand LPs behaviour and get a better understanding of related topics such as price impact, slippage, impermanent loss, etc..
Addtionally, this page also provides a comparison between different pricing models, and attempts to showcase why a particular pricing model may be better suited for a specific type of asset (eg. stablecoins.).
Finally, this page attempts to aggregate several resources with usefull information about LP's and Dexes.
